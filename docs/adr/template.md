# ADR Template

## Context
- Describe the problem, constraints, and triggering conditions.

## Decision
- State the decision in specific, falsifiable terms.
- For Accepted ADRs, use present tense only.

## Alternatives Considered
- List real alternatives and why they were not chosen.

## Consequences
- Operational impact (runtime, storage, tooling, support).
- Maintenance impact (ownership, cost of change, required updates).

## Trade-offs
- Explicitly note what is gained and what is sacrificed.

## Status
- Proposed | Accepted | Superseded | Deprecated

## Date
- YYYY-MM-DD
